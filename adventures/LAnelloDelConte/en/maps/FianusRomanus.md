# Fianus Romanus

Village in southern Drakenhold, connected to the capital. Surrounded by fertile lands, it faces the sea to the south.

## Quarters and Interior Locations

- **Mercato Centrale** — main square; the Taberna de Franciosibus aut Hispanardis is located here
- **Aviano** — south-east quarter; the Count's residence (closed, guarded)
- **Settebagni** — quarter south of the Mercato Centrale and south-west of Aviano; sewer entrance under Terzilio the greengrocer's cart
- **Ponte Milvius** — connects the Mercato to Settebagni
- **Tempio** — near the sewer entrance

## Geographic Notes

- Fianus Romanus is on flat ground, surrounded by fertile lands, facing the sea to the south
- **Vetusta Portus** — seaside hamlet south of Fianus, the port. Merchandise arrives here by sea (and so does La Contessina).
- The roads leading to the city pass over the walls forming bridges (hence the name "Ponte Milvio")
- There's a quarter called Ponte Milvio that isn't near the actual bridge — the toponymy of Fianus is intentionally absurd
- The Count's residence is visible from almost the entire village but nobody ever enters
- The sewers drain from east (Aviano) toward west (Settebagni) — the entrance is in Settebagni
