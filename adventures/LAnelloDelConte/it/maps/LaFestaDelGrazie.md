# La Festa del Grazie — Mappa della piazza del Mercato Centrale

Piazza principale di Fianus Romanus durante la Festa del Grazie.

## Immagine

`LaFestaDelGrazie.png`

## Prompt Gemini

Top-down battle map of a medieval town square during a harvest festival. The square is roughly rectangular, cobblestone ground.

Around the edges: stone buildings on all four sides (tavern to the north with a boar sign, blacksmith east, residential buildings with porticos south, small municipal building west).

In the square: a small wooden stage/platform (north side, for speeches), a stone fountain with a boar statue in the center, 6-8 colorful market stalls arranged in loose rows, 3-4 wooden carts (one with vegetables parked southwest), barrels and crates scattered around. Festive decorations: colored banners hanging between buildings, garlands.

Warm daylight, no people or tokens. Fantasy medieval style. Grid overlay with 5ft squares. Top-down view, D&D battle map style.
