## Description

Corrupt governor of Orcastle, Lord Malebranche is a thin, pale man with sunken eyes that seem to calculate the value of every person in the room. He wears dark noble garments with silver embroidery. His hands are always cold — a side effect of the necromantic magic he practices.

Distinctive trait: he speaks with impeccable courtesy even when ordering atrocities. He never raises his voice. His calm is more unsettling than any threat.

## Motivations

Malebranche built his power on the magical dome that protects Orcastle. He controls the city with an iron fist, but his position is more fragile than it appears: he has lost the Scroll of Judgment, and without it his dominion is incomplete.
