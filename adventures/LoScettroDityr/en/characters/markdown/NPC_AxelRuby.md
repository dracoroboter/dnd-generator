## Description

A massive man, arms covered in arena scars. Square jaw, nose broken multiple times, small calculating eyes that betray an intelligence his physique doesn't suggest. He wears a studded leather vest over a worn shirt and a rough ruby hanging from his neck — the gang's symbol.

He speaks little and in a low voice, forcing his interlocutors to lean in. When he laughs, it's a short, unpleasant sound. He has a habit of cracking his knuckles before making a decision.

Distinctive trait: brutal and cunning in equal measure. He's not the stupid brute he appears to be — he's a seasoned smuggler who learned to survive in the arena and on the streets.

## Motivations

Axel wants total control of Orcastle. He hates Miranda Emerald with visceral ferocity — he considers her a manipulator who fights with tricks and poisons instead of face to face. His immediate goal is to eliminate the Emeralds and consolidate Ruby dominance over the entire city.

As a former vassal of Malebranche, he knows the dynamics of feudal power. He left that world for the streets, but retains the ambition of someone who grew up close to the throne.

## Role in the Adventure

Axel is one of the three faction leaders in Orcastle. The PCs encounter him in the eastern quarter, Ruby territory.

**Trust test**: to earn Axel's trust, the PCs must bring him an Emerald member — alive or dead. He doesn't accept excuses, doesn't accept promises. He wants results.

**Module D**: after the taking of Orcastle, Axel becomes a vassal of Count Wildforge (Kreig Highlander). He cooperates with the PCs, albeit reluctantly.

## DM Notes

- Axel **doesn't know** that Ombrascura is hiding among his men. This is critical information: if the PCs reveal it to him, his reaction depends on how they discovered it and how much he trusts them.
- The rivalry with Miranda is personal and irreconcilable — he will never accept an alliance with her, unless forced by an existential threat.
- In combat, Axel is direct: he charges the most dangerous enemy. He doesn't flee easily — the arena taught him that turning your back means certain death.
- If the PCs choose to ally with the Emeralds, Axel becomes a serious obstacle but not a main villain.
