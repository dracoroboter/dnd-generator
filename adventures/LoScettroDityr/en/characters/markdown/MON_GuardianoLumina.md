# Guardian of Lumina

## General Information

- **Role**: temple guardian, potential ally
- **Race**: construct (stone golem)
- **Alignment**: Neutral (corrupted by Vecna)

## Description

A huge crouching stone golem, clearly wounded — deep cracks run through its body and stone fragments are missing from its torso and arms. It was a sacred guardian of the Temple of Lumina, now pervaded by Vecna's dark influence. An aura of necromantic energy surrounds it.

It does not speak except in single words and monosyllables. It has a rough understanding of human language, not intelligent enough to hold a conversation. It recognizes bearers of the Scepter and their companions as friends and defends them from anyone who claims to want to take it from them.
