# Sunken Sanctuary of Ostegard — DM Map

## Overview

Ancient temple submerged by water, located in a coastal cavern south of Orcastle. Not marked on any maps. Zikle provides a rough description; Kreig Wildforge can provide a small boat to reach it. The sanctuary houses the **Monile d'Oro di Ostegard**, a talisman needed for the ritual to dissolve Malebranche's curse.

The atmosphere is oppressive: walls and floor drip with moisture, the sanctuary is partially flooded.

### Antechamber (entrance)

- **Description:** Descending staircase. Sanctuary partially flooded. Two stone figures standing motionless in the dark water.
- **Contents:** 2 **Stone Golem** (HP 178, AC 17, +10, 3d8+6 slam, CR 5, Immutable, Magic Resistance).
- **Hazards:** HARD encounter for 5-6 PCs lv10.
- **Exits:** Back (staircase to the outside), Forward (sealed door → Central chamber).

### Sealed Door

- **Description:** Enormous door with three sacred seals. Text written in Common on the door.
- **Mechanics:** Arcana DC 12 to understand the magical meaning of the inscription and the connection between the inscription and the immovable door. The door has no mechanical lock and cannot be opened by any other means than pronouncing the oath.
- **Oath:** *"I will fight the death that walks upon the earth and I shall be protected."*
- **Effect of the oath:** Whoever pronounces the oath opens the door. They emanate an aura that weakens undead in melee with them (vulnerable to all their attacks, lose all immunities, duration: 24 hours). Undead attack them first.
- **Heavy door:** Athletics DC 15 to open it after pronouncing the oath.

### Central Chamber (Monile chamber)

- **Description:** Walls of carved stone with engravings and frescoes of forgotten legends, illuminated by flickering light. At the center, a pedestal adorned with arcane symbols.
- **Contents:** The **Monile d'Oro di Ostegard** rests on the pedestal (blue and silver hues, seems to pulse with a life of its own).
- **Guardian:** **Sir Aldric** (Sword Wraith, CR 8) appears from the shadows. Pale face, melancholic eyes. Poses a moral trial to the PCs before granting the Monile.

#### Sir Aldric's Moral Trial

Sir Aldric says: *"Many call themselves righteous. But the weight of their actions reveals the truth. You have come to claim the Monile, but are you ready to pay the price of your mission? Choose wisely. Do you wish to absolve them, condemn them, or sacrifice yourselves for them?"*

Three spirits of the PCs' recent victims manifest:

1. **The Coerced Bandit** — A poor man who stole to feed his family. Asks: *"Did I truly deserve this condemnation?"*
2. **The Fanatical Cultist** — An acolyte of Dispater, trained since childhood. Asks: *"Is it just to judge a man for the path he could never see?"*
3. **The Corrupt Noble** — A cruel lord (may be Othran Vorash). Sneering: *"I regret nothing. But tell me, didn't you kill me out of convenience? How much justice was in your hands, and how much vengeance?"*

For each spirit, a designated member of the group must respond by choosing between:
- **Absolve** — Grant peace. Demonstrates mercy.
- **Condemn** — Declare the choice just. Demonstrates determination.
- **Sacrifice** — A PC takes upon themselves part of the curse.

If the answers are all sincere and they accept the consequences, the door opens and the Monile is accessible. If they lie or refuse, Sir Aldric attacks. To evaluate sincerity: based on roleplay, or Deception DC 18 if they explicitly declare they are lying.

## DM Notes

### Final Flooding

Once the Monile is obtained, the Sanctuary begins to be submerged permanently. The PCs must flee quickly.

### Access to the Sanctuary

- Not marked on any maps. Zikle gives a rough description.
- To reach it: Nature DC 14 to navigate. If they fail, they get lost and encounter 4 Ghoul (CR 1).
- Kreig Wildforge can provide a small boat.
- Coastal cavern south of Orcastle.

### The Moral Trial — Narrative Heart

There is no "right" answer. All three choices (absolve, condemn, sacrifice) are valid as long as they are sincere. The DM must evaluate sincerity based on roleplay. This is not a mechanical puzzle but a moment of character introspection.

### Connection to the Final Battle

After the Sanctuary, the PCs head to Zikle's tomb in the Misty Forest for the ritual with the Monile. Malebranche reaches them there for the final battle.
