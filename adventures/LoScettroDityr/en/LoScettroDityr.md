# The Scepter of Tyr

## Lore

**Lo Scettro di Tyr** is a divine weapon said to have been wielded by Tyr himself. Only the pure can use it without suffering the curse that consumes the soul and transforms the bearer into an undead servant. The Scepter was lost during the **Fall of the Temple of Lumina**, when a priest corrupted by Vecna attempted to steal it. **Ormut**, a spellcaster devoted to Tyr, teleported it to an unknown location — the **Kingdom of Erythale**, an ethereal plane of light and crystal — before dying at the hands of the traitors.

Since then, the Scepter has become the stuff of legends. **Vecna**, the God of Secrets, wants it to amplify his dark power and transform humanity into undead servants. The **Custodi dell'Equilibrio**, an order dedicated to Tyr, want to recover it for their leader **Sir Alaric il Giusto** — who hides personal ambitions of absolute power.

The saga takes place along the Sword Coast and beyond, from **Orcastle** (a prison-city under a magical dome) to the frozen lands of **Svoalbard** north of Bryn Shander.

## Introduction

> "I owe you my life. My wrath shall fall upon you if you do not begin searching for Lo Scettro di Tyr. Immeasurable rewards will be bestowed upon you if you accomplish the task."

The characters find themselves caught in a chain of events that takes them from forced service to a tyrant (Malebranche) to a mission on behalf of Vecna, until they must choose sides in a conflict between deities. Their moral choices determine the fate of entire kingdoms.

The adventure begins with the PCs being kidnapped by **Lord Cedric Malebranche**, who forces them to recover the **Pergamena del Giudizio** in the prison-city of Orcastle. From there, a pact with **Vecna** sends them on a quest for Lo Scettro di Tyr. The journey takes them through mountains, ruined temples, and alternate planes of existence, to the final showdown.

## General Plot

- **Module A — Escape from Orcastle**: the PCs, kidnapped and fitted with explosive collars, must recover the Pergamena del Giudizio in the prison-city of Orcastle and find a way to escape. Four factions control the island, each with their own secrets.
- **Module B — Lo Scettro di Tyr**: Vecna tasks the PCs with recovering the Scepter from the Temple of Lumina. Journey from Baldur's Gate through dangerous mountains, trials of purity in the temple, and a final confrontation with Alaric il Giusto.
- **Module C — Return Home**: the PCs must deliver the Scepter to the altar of Tyr in Svoalbard. Sea voyage along the Sword Coast, pursued by a spy of Vecna and the warlock Othran Vorash.
- **Module D — The End Does Not Belong to the Dead**: Malebranche, defeated, summons the wizard Zikle and curses the PCs. The dead rise wherever they go. They must find the Monile d'Oro di Ostegard to break the curse and defeat Malebranche once and for all.

## DM Advice

- **Tone**: dark fantasy with moments of moral tension. The PCs start on the side of evil (working for Vecna) and must earn their redemption. Moral choices have real mechanical consequences.
- **Continuity**: the modules are sequential. Characters like Frankie, Ombrascura, Kreig, Miranda, and Ruby return in different modules. Keep track of relationships.
- **Vecna**: is always present as a threat. If the PCs ignore his requests, they suffer progressive curses (d6 table in Module B). He is not an enemy to fight directly — he is a puppeteer.
- **The Scepter**: is an extremely powerful but dangerous object. Tyr's curse strikes anyone who touches it without being pure. Only the designated herald can wield it. Keep track of who carries it and the consequences.
- **Difficulty**: the adventure scales from lv8 (Module A) to lv10 (Modules C-D). Encounters are calibrated for 4-6 PCs. Module A has many non-combat options; Module D is the most combat-heavy.

## Key Items

### La Pergamena del Giudizio (Module A)

Contains the spell to bring down the magical dome of Orcastle. Protected by a curse: only a paladin or a character pure of heart can activate it (plus the secret word known to Ombrascura). Also includes a message spell with the password "Zikle l'Illuminato".

### Lo Scettro di Tyr (Modules B-C)

Divine crystal weapon. Properties: Sacred Resurrection (1 charge), Empowerment of Justice (+2 to hit, necrotic/psychic resistance, advantage vs fear within 30 feet), Shield of Purity (1/day, halves damage for 3 turns), Sentence of Tyr (1/day, paralysis + truth). Curse: anyone who touches it without Tyr's consent suffers nightmares, halved healing, maximum HP halved each day until death or repentance.

### La Coppa di Blackwood (Module A)

Artifact in the Torre di Torth. Functions as Dispel Magic (powerful enough for the collars). Magic word: "Obloc Vecnal" (Praise Vecna in Infernal).

### Il Monile d'Oro di Ostegard (Module D)

Talisman that channels sacred energy to disperse necromantic corruption. Kept in the Sunken Sanctuary of Ostegard, guarded by Sir Aldric (sword wraith).
