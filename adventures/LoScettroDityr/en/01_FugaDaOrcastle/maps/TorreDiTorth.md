# Tower of Torth — DM Map

## Overview

Ancient temple dedicated to Vecna, built by the priest Ablunth Torth as a monument to dark power. Located in the northern area of the island of Orcastle, beyond the rocky plain infested by cultists and devils. The tower is made of black stone, apparently uninhabited, with a spiral of steep stairs. The air is thick with magical energy and the walls bear inscriptions in a lost language.

The structure has three levels: entrance/hall on the ground floor, altar room at the top, and underground dungeon accessible through a well that opens with a ritual.

**Trial 1 — Hall of Reflection:**
- Floor polished like a mirror.
- The PCs' doubles emerge from the floor and attack.
- Runes on the opposite wall with the phrase: "The shadow is nothing without the light, the shadow has the power you give it."
- Insight or Arcana DC 15 to understand that ignoring the doubles is the solution.
- The doubles use only basic attacks and cantrips, same HP/AC as the PCs, vulnerable to psychic damage, do not heal.

**Trial 2 — Labyrinth of Vecna:**
- Checkerboard floor with letters on each tile.
- Walk on the correct letters to spell "V.E.C.N.A."
- Wrong letter: poisoned dart, 2d4+2 piercing damage to all (Dexterity saving throw DC 12 halves).

**Trial 3 — Guardian of the Portal:**
- Final room with the Teleportation Portal (circle of runic stones).
- **Stone Golem** (HP 178, AC 17, +10, 3d8+6 slam) guards the portal.
- Combat or Deception/Persuasion DC 18 to convince it you are the new custodians.
- Fresco on the wall: open portal and a cleric drinking from a cup.

**The Teleportation Portal:**
- Requires the Coppa di Blackwood.
- Arcana or History DC 15 to understand the rite.
- Toasting with the cup in honor of Vecna activates the portal.
- Teleports to the vicinity of Malebranche's palace on the mainland.

## DM Notes

### Ritual to Open the Well (Level 2 → Level 3)

At least **2 out of 3 steps** must succeed:

1. **Offer blood** — A PC offers blood on the altar (1d6 necrotic damage). The blood is absorbed, the basin emits a red glow.
2. **Evoke the past** — Tell aloud a memory or promise. Persuasion or Religion DC 13. If they fail, the statues come to life and attack.
3. **Light the sacred flames** — A fire spell (Fireball, Burning Hands, or similar) on the ashes in the basin. The gems in the statues glow, a loud noise from the foundations.

### Vecna's Apparition (Level 2)

- Appears when the Coppa di Blackwood is picked up and the inscription is read.
- It is an avatar: cannot be hit and cannot hit.
- Proposes a pact: will remove the collars in exchange for a future mission (unspecified).
- If they accept, reveals that the cup functions as Dispel Magic. Magic word: "Obloc Vecnal" (Praise Vecna in Infernal).
- The PCs can also discover the function with Arcana DC 15. Detect Magic reveals only abjuration.

### Doubles in the Hall of Reflection

Run as simplified copies: only basic attacks (main weapon) and cantrips, no spell slots or class features. Same HP and AC but no active magic items. Damage dealt by the doubles is psychic. Detect Magic reveals illusion. The shadows disappear after wounding at least three characters. The goal is the discovery that ignoring them is the solution.

### Access to the Northern Area

Before reaching the tower, the PCs must cross the rocky plain to the north with three sequential encounters:
1. 4 cult fanatics (CR 2) — EASY
2. 2 bearded devils (CR 5) + 2 imps (CR 1) invisible — MEDIUM
3. 1 chain devil (CR 8) + surviving imps — HARD

The plain offers partial or total cover (1d4: 1-3 partial, 4 total).
