Sentient Item. 

Jason exists as a soul in the Ring of the Virtuoso. He has no physical body. He communicates telepathically with the wearer. The stats above represent his mental and magical capabilities, not a body.

Spellcasting (as lv10 bard). 

spell save DC 16, spell attack +8. Slots: 4/3/3/3/2. Jason can only cast spells through the wearer (when he has control).

Supreme Manipulator. 

advantage on Deception and Persuasion checks. Creatures that speak with Jason (through the wearer) have disadvantage on saves against his charm spells.

Progressive Possession. 

see "Progressive possession" section above. Jason doesn't fight directly — he takes control of the wearer.

Actions

Psychic Wave (when a 1 comes up on the d8). 

Saving throw: none (automatic) Damage: 1d4 + grip level psychic damage Area: all creatures within 15m / 50ft / 10qd of the wearer Effect: hits allies and enemies indiscriminately

Mental Conflict (when Jason forces control). 

Saving throw: Wisdom DC 14-16+ (varies with grip) Failure: Jason takes control of the wearer for 1d4 rounds (combat) or 1d4 hours (out of combat) Success: the wearer resists, grip decreases by 1

Nightly Nightmares (grip 3+). 

Damage: 2d6 psychic per night Effect: no benefit from long rest
